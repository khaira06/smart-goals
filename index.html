SMART Goal: From January 1, 2025, to December 31, 2025, we will improve our HDI SCS Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) score by 5 percentage points (from 86% to 91%) by focusing on better communication, agent coaching, and consistent follow-up with end users.

Measurement: Progress will be tracked monthly through HDI SCS survey results and quarterly reports.

Relevant: Customer satisfaction is one of the most important HDI SCS indicators of service quality and directly reflects how end users view the help desk.
